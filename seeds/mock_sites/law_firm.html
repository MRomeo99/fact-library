About Mitchell & Associates

Mitchell & Associates has represented clients in Houston and across Texas since 1998. Our team of 8 attorneys has recovered over $50 million in settlements and verdicts for our clients. We operate on a contingency fee basis for personal injury cases — you pay nothing unless we win.
