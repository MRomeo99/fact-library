Consultation and Fees

How much does an initial consultation cost?
Initial consultations are $150 for up to 60 minutes. The fee is fully credited toward
your retainer if you retain us. Free 15-minute phone consultations are available for
personal injury inquiries only.

Do you work on contingency?
Yes — personal injury cases are handled on a contingency fee basis. You pay nothing
unless we win your case. Our standard contingency fee is 33% of the settlement amount,
or 40% if the case goes to trial.

What is a retainer?
For hourly-rate matters (family law, estate planning, business litigation), we collect
a retainer upfront. Retainers range from $2,500 to $10,000 depending on case complexity.
We bill at $275–$395/hour depending on which attorney handles your matter.

Intake Process

What should I bring to my first appointment?
- Any documents related to your case (accident reports, medical records, contracts, etc.)
- A photo ID
- Insurance information (if applicable)
- A list of questions you want answered

How long does the intake process take?
The initial intake form takes about 15 minutes to complete. Please arrive 10 minutes
early to complete paperwork, or complete it online at our client portal before your visit.

What happens after the consultation?
If you decide to retain us, we will send an engagement letter within 24 hours. Once
signed and the retainer received, we begin work immediately.

Communication and Updates

How often will I hear from my attorney?
We commit to returning all client calls and emails within one business day. For active
litigation, you will receive monthly status updates.

Do you have an after-hours emergency line?
For criminal matters and emergency family law situations (domestic violence, emergency
custody), call our after-hours line at (214) 555-0100.
